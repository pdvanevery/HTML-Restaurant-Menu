Origin Story

H-Thai-ML was founded in San Jose, CA and has been delivering exceptional service since 2010. We founded this restaruant in hopes to appeal to the technology driven nature of Silicon Valley; as former coders ourselves, we know how long the hours can be and how much of a drag cooking can be, so we try to make it as easy as can be.
